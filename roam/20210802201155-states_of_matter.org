#+title: States of Matter
1. Solid: fixed shape and does not conform to the container
  - particles lie next to each other in a regular array (3d pattern)
2. Liquid: varying shape that conforms to the container, but only to the extend
  of the liquids volume
  - particles lie close together but move randomly around each other
3. Gas: Varying shape but does not have a surface, and is not bound to its volume
  - particles have large distances and move randomly throughout the container
